Worker-Owned Cooperative

Working to Improve the Lives of Everyday Americans

Breathe is a worker-owned cooperative dedicated to giving everyday Americans the data and tools to understand how the system really works — and how to change it.

🏛️

What we Believe

We believe democratic governments should represent the people and serve their interests. That capitalism should be based on competition, choice, and fair rules — not rigged by those at the top.

🎯

The Question

The question is not Democrat or Republican, liberal or conservative, progressive or moderate. The question is, rule by the people or rule by corporations and billionaires.

The Problem

A System Hijacked Over 50 Years

A small group of ultra-wealthy interests has implemented a fantastically successful plan: legalizing the purchasing of politicians, owning the media narrative, appointing judges who favor big business over citizens, and shaping our very understanding of markets through textbooks and university programs.

Their goals have always been about accumulating money and power — legalizing and creating monopolies to exploit workers, consumers, suppliers, and our land.

Learn how they did it The Master Plan, why they did it American Character, how it affects you Break Em Up, and how our current system works The System

The Path Forward

Taking America Back

The only way forward is to change the system itself: get money out of politics for good, remove lobbyists from the legislative process, create term limits, and build true representation of the people instead of big money.

Breathe exists to make that case with data — clearly, honestly, and with a lazer focus on achieving government of the people, by the people, and for the people.
